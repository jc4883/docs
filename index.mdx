Muscat is a CLI tool that connects to the Twitter/X API v2 to pull your home timeline and AI-focused keyword searches, then produces a curated digest of the most substantive AI content. Run it on demand with a single slash command in Claude Code, or schedule it to deliver twice-daily summaries automatically.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to Twitter/X">
    Add your Twitter API credentials to `.env`. Muscat uses OAuth 1.0a for home timeline access and a Bearer Token for keyword searches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fetch your feed">
    Run `/summarize-ai-news` in Claude Code (or call the CLI directly) to pull your home timeline and AI-relevant search results from the past 6 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your digest">
    Muscat scores and ranks tweets by engagement quality, resolves full quote and reply chains, and presents a structured digest with sections for Deep Reads, Research, Industry Moves, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay in the loop">
    Schedule Muscat to run at 8am and 5pm daily so you never miss important AI developments in your network.
  </Step>
</Steps>
